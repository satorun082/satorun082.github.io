Methods work well

  • Ensemble of big models(ViT)
  • Iterative pseudo-labeling
  • Class inbalance training
  • Clustering or contrastive learning for self-supervised learning

Methods do not work well

  • SoTA semi-supervised methods(e.g. FixMatch, MixMatch, UDA)
  • Online methods for pseudo label or consistency training
